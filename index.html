About Us

The Quiets are a personal data collection and silent observation agency born in 2015 out of the Facts and Fictions in the Age of Data program at School of Machines, Making & Make-Believe.

Our Work

We are an artist collective that creates data art based on silent observations of experiences, conversations, parties and other events big and small.
